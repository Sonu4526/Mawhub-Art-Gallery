About

Founder of Mawhub Art Gallery Roohy Khan is an Artist/Arabic Calligrapher and a Curator based in Saudi Arabia basically from City Bhopal,India . Member of Saudi Arabian Society for Culture and Arts (SASCA) . Participated in 34 exhibitions including both Solo and Group in various countries including India , UAE , Saudi Arabia,  Qatar , Pakistan .

National Awardee .She got National Award in All India Calligraphy Competition and Exhibition (in 2014) held at M.F.Hussain Art Gallery in New Delhi, India. She have Curated and Organised 4 Art Exhibitions in Riyadh KSA . On part of academics , She have  done Masters in Fine Arts and Masters in Biotechnology. To further excel her Artistic skills, later done Diploma in Calligraphy and Graphic Design from Aligarh Muslim University, Aligarh. Some of her Artworks are part of Government Museums in India .

It all started with a dream she had seen when I was planning to continue my studies further in Biotechnology, the dream was that she was doing Arabic Calligraphy and was a turning point in her profession career and prompted her to chose Art field and specifically Arabic Calligraphy.
